Annelies Marie "Anne" Frank

1929-1945

Who is Anne Frank?

German diarist of Jewish heritage
One of the most-discussed Jewish victims of the Holocaust

Timeline

  • 1929 - Born on June 12, 1929 in Frankfrut, Germany.
  • 1933 - Frank family moves to Amsterdam, Holland to escape the frowing persecution in Germany.
  • 1940 - Germany invades Netherlands.
  • 1942 - Anne receives a diary for her 13th birthday.
    July 6, 1942 Frank family moves into the secret annex.
  • 1944 - The occupants at the secret annex are arrested and sent to Westerbork transit camp.
    The occupants of the annex are transported to Auschwitz-Birkenau concentration camp on the last train to leave Westerbork.
    • Anne Frank and her sister, Margot, are transported to Bergen-Belsen concentration camp in October.
  • 1945 - Anne dies of typhus, a few weeks before the camp is liberated.
  • 1947 - Anne's diary is first published.
